The unit volume decrease was offset mildly by a 0.9% increase in the average selling price of wire per copper pound sold.

Net income for the fourth quarter of 2018 increased to $25 million versus $23.7 million in the third quarter of 2018. EPS was $1.20 in the fourth quarter of 2018 versus $1.13 in the third quarter of 2018.
