This amazing portfolio of iconic hotels generated an ADR of $283, RevPAR of $239, and EBITDA per key of a rich $41,000 in 2018. As we look to the future, we expect to continue to pursue and acquire additional iconic hotels that will expand this very unique portfolio.

Our Urban Contemporary portfolio includes 27 hotels located in 10 different cities.
