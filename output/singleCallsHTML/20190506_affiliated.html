The evolution in the asset management industry present opportunities for us. Client appetite for truly differentiated, independently managed return streams is enduring. Aligning interest between AMG or Affiliates in their clients and building strong enduring Affiliate relationships over multiple generations.

As we focus on these competitive strengths, consistent with our core principles of entrepreneurialism, alignment and long-term orientation, I'm confident that we will capitalize on the opportunities before us to broaden the scale, diversity, and earnings power of our business and generate long-term shareholder value.
