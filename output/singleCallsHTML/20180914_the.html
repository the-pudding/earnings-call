Our products are sold through our tech-enabled experiential showrooms that allow the customer to touch, feel, and see the product before purchasing. Beginning two years ago we began to pivot from a traditional retail-store model to a showroom model. Given the success we've seen in the new prototype showroom, going forward, we plan to remodel all of our legacy stores into the prototype showrooms I just discussed. Currently, just under 50% of our total showroom fleet is in the legacy format and for fiscal 2019 we plan to remodel up to a total of 10 additional showrooms.

We see significant white-space opportunity for our showrooms and we will continue to expand our showroom presence, with 15 openings planned for fiscal 2019, ending with 78 showrooms by the end of the year. Given the strong unit economics, we plan to continue to open more showrooms, which will also help us to increase our brand awareness and improve the efficiency of our marketing.
