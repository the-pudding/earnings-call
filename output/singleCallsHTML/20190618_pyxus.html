For the fiscal year ended March 31, 2019, full-service volumes increased 4.9% to 400.5 million kilos, despite the impact of Hurricane Florence and foreign tariffs on U.S.

tobacco. These decreases were partially offset by an increase in leaf volume mainly attributable to larger crops in Africa. These increases were partially offset by leaf cost savings and restructuring initiatives. SG&A as a percentage of sales increased from 8% for the year ended March 31, 2018 to 9.6% for the year ended March 31, 2019.
