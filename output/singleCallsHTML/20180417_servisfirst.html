If there's a market that doesn't have much C&I, that's probably not a good market for us, no matter how good the people are.

The good people in a non C&I market, they're commercial real estate people for the most part. So that's just not a fit for us and you and I both know the commercial real estate type of customers don't generate a lot of deposits to go along with it.
